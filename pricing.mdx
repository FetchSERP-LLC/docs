## Example Cost Calculation

Suppose you:

* Fetch 3 pages of Google SERP results → `3 × 1 = 3 credits`
* Get ranking data for 5 keywords (single page each) → `5 × 1 = 5 credits`
* Run a full web page SEO analysis → `5 credits`

**Total** = **13 credits** (≈ **$0.013**)
